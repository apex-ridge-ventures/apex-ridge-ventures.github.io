The Problem

Traditional publishing faces a fundamental challenge: once printed, books become static. Any updates, corrections, or additional content require expensive reprints and redistribution. QR codes in books quickly become obsolete, leading to dead links and frustrated readers.

Our Solution

Minz revolutionizes how publishers and authors connect with their readers through dynamic QR codes. Our platform enables:

  • Dynamic Content Updates: Change destinations without reprinting
  • Reader Analytics: Track engagement and usage patterns
  • Cost Savings: Eliminate expensive reprint cycles
  • Enhanced Reader Experience: Always-current, relevant content

Key Features

Dynamic QR Codes

Generate QR codes that can be updated anytime without changing the printed code. Perfect for books, magazines, and marketing materials.

Reader Analytics

Comprehensive analytics dashboard showing scan rates, geographic distribution, device types, and reader engagement patterns.

Mobile Optimized

Destination pages are automatically optimized for mobile devices, ensuring the best reader experience across all platforms.

Enterprise Security

Bank-level security with encrypted QR codes, access controls, and audit trails for enterprise publishing workflows.
